Zaril Nanaz

The Nerd Brother - Psychology Master

Background, Personality & Motivations

Identity and Background

Basic Information

Zaril Nanaz is one of the two main protagonists in Revival: Lost, alongside his brother Liraz. Both brothers are 60 years old and have been separated since they were 10 due to their intense rivalry.

Education

Zaril holds a PhD in Psychology and has a psychology master's degree, making him the intellectual "nerd brother" of the duo.

The Separation

The brothers' rivalry was so intense they were "about to nuke the world to win." Responsible parents separated them for 50 years. They are eventually reunited by The Diabolic, who uses their current trauma and places money around to bring them back together.

Personality Traits

Zaril is portrayed as the nerd brother, the smart one of the two. He has an extensive vocabulary and knowledge though he is slightly rigid. He uses his smarts to outmaneuver his brother Liraz at every opportunity he gets. He also has experience in the sword and culinary arts. He enjoys smiling over his defeated brother.

Zaril is portrayed as conspiring and focused on farming wishes from the Genie to create "ludicrous stuff for violence." He's initially skeptical, stating he won't accept the Genie's existence until he has a "portable version of Neptune legally under his name."

The Genie nicknames him "nerdillious Wierdo", which he objects to strongly, even challenging her to a duel over it. He enjoys conflict as a "perfect distraction for Liraz" and takes winning very seriously in competitions.

Core Motivation

Zaril's primary motivation is his rivalry with Liraz. Their obsession with competing against each other is literally what keeps them alive in the haunted house. The game explores the theme: "What is the result of obsession?"

If one brother dies, the other also dies because without the competition, they lose their reason to avoid the ghosts. They need competition, not cooperation, to survive.

Gameplay & Abilities

Combat System

Weapon Mastery

Zaril uses a Katana and Nata duo to defeat enemies with these combat moves:

  • Stinger attack - Precise thrusting technique
  • Jump slash - Aerial sword combat
  • Throwing his Nata - Ranged weapon attack (must be reclaimed afterward)

DJ Mechanics

Psychology-Based Audio Control

Utilizing his psychology degree, Zaril employs music as a tactical weapon:

  • Anxiety-inducing music - Scares the ghosts
  • Funky music - Distracts the ghosts

Trash Talk System

Verbal Combat Mechanics

Zaril and Liraz use trash talk as a survival mechanic against ghosts:

  • Utter ignorance - Causes panic
  • Quipping - Creates confusion
  • Insulting - Disrupts ghost behavior
  • Breakdancing - Disorients enemies

Upgradeable Stats:

  • Vocabulary
  • Volume control
  • Delivery Timing

Note: Trash talking certain family members can trigger protective frenzies from other ghosts.

Genie Wish System

Zaril can "farm wishes" from the Genie, using them as major moves to obtain resources and items like bazookas or his coveted portable Neptune.

Notable Competition Moments

Uno Victory

Zaril wins using a "swap decks" card and reminds Liraz that he didn't say "Uno," showcasing their competitive and insulting dynamic.

Connect 4 Triumph

Uses his psychology degree as a taunt during the match, demonstrating how he leverages his education as psychological warfare.

Game Endings & Lore Role

Zaril can be the protagonist who escapes, cures family members, or interacts with the Mother in various game endings. However, in certain bad endings, if he goes "too deep" in the secret parts of the game, The Diabolic will take him as his vessel to destroy the universe.

In the "Bevrious" sequence, if Zaril and Liraz reach the Sinus section, they are dismembered and die. At the CANON end of the game, both brothers eventually join a multiversal anti-diabolic group.

Media Presence

Zaril is featured in the game's main theme song, described as a rap battle between him and Liraz. He's also depicted on the potential box art in a sword fight with his brother. The game's creator, Zapped Zaid, hopes to voice act Zaril personally.
